Stank Fitness

ben bostick - Personal Trainer

My story starts back in High School, where my football career had garnered the attention of several college programs. As fate would have it, a broken ankle and torn ligaments would interfere with those opportunities and I was left only with a scholarship to the Naval Academy.

I spent a year as a student-athlete for the Naval Academy Prep School but was expelled due to poor decisions off the field. A second chance presented itself with the football program of Campbellsville University in Kentucky. But, my performance lacked and I began to lose confidence in myself and my path.

I ended up in Lexington, Kentucky where I had friends playing for its football program. Here I regressed into a life driven by weekend partying that would soon spill into weekday benders. A job as a bouncer at a local bar quickly evolved into a bartending job where everything I did revolved around alcohol and its consumption. A nasty escape cycle from my personal problems.

It would take 10 years of this depression and stagnant living before I slowly made changes. A business opportunity with a good friend in Knoxville, Tennessee allowed me to change my environment and outlook.

In this chapter, I returned to physical activity. Cleansing and training my body, mind and spirit. I understand the value of my progress and how it can be shared to help others. Stank Fitness is the embodiment of overcoming and pushing through whatever obstacles currently linger in your life. I want to help you prepare or maintain for whatever circumstances you have through the physical and mental conditioning of the body. Let’s work hard and make stank faces together!
